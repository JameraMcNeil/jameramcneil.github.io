Learn to Play Black Jeopardy

Enjoy this special version of Jeopardy that quizes you on the intricacies of Black culture.

Select a dollar ammount within one of five categories that speaks to you. After reading the question that pops up, select your answer from a list of answers!

If the answer is correct, you earn that dollar amount and can move on to the next question. If the answer is incorrect, you will not recieve the the money. You have four chances for a new round.
